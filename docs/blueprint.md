# **App Name**: NeuroFlow AI

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Hero Section: A visually striking hero area with a clear, concise headline about the AI chatbot and a primary call-to-action (CTA) button to drive engagement, encouraging users to explore further or try the AI.
- Interactive AI Demo Module: An embedded, basic chat interface allowing visitors to interact directly with the AI chatbot's core function. This acts as a 'tool' for quick, guided prompts to showcase its capabilities.
- Benefit Highlight Sections: Clearly presented, scannable sections that articulate the key advantages and solutions offered by the AI chatbot, using concise text and evocative visuals to convey value.
- How It Works Explanation: An intuitive, step-by-step visual explanation of how users can start using or benefiting from the AI chatbot, simplifying the onboarding process.
- User Testimonials Carousel: A rotating display of positive feedback and social proof from early adopters or conceptual users to build trust and credibility for the AI chatbot.

## Style Guidelines:

- Dark mode background: A deep, sophisticated bluish-grey (#141619) that evokes modernity and focus, setting a calm yet impactful stage for the content.
- Primary accent color: A vibrant electric blue (#1C8AFF) for interactive elements and key highlights, providing a striking contrast against the dark background, conveying technological innovation and clarity.
- Secondary accent color: A rich, expressive purple (#6940E8) to complement the primary blue, used for subtle flair, secondary call-to-actions, and differentiating important information, enhancing the 'neon' effect requested.
- Headlines and body text: 'Space Grotesk', a sans-serif typeface, selected for its modern, slightly techy, and clean aesthetic, providing excellent readability in a digital context.
- Abstract, geometric, and clean line icons to reinforce the AI-driven, modern, and sophisticated feel of the application.
- Segmented, full-width sections for a clean and impactful presentation, with generous negative space to enhance focus on key information and CTAs. The layout prioritizes a strong hero area and clear hierarchy for other sections.
- Subtle and fluid animations on scroll-in for sections and hover effects for interactive elements, contributing to a polished user experience without being distracting. Light pulsing effects could highlight the interactive AI demo.
